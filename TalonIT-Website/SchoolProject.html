Empowering Schools Through Connectivity

Talon IT Solutions is actively working with schools across Mamelodi to install high-speed, secure, and affordable Wi-Fi networks. This project is designed to support digital learning and create a stronger educational infrastructure for students and staff alike.

What We Provide:

  • Site survey and signal coverage planning
  • Installation of routers and Wi-Fi access points
  • Secure network setup with password protection
  • Device connection for classrooms and admin offices
  • Post-installation testing and optimization
  • 1-week after-installation remote technical support

Timeline

  • Site Visit & Assessment – 1 Day
  • Equipment Procurement – 1–2 Days
  • Installation & Configuration – 1–2 Days
  • Post-installation Support – 1 Week (remote)

Total Duration: 3–5 business days
